## Social Platform Features {#social}

<img width="200" src="/images/destinations-oc6.png" />
The Meta Horizon platform offers a number of features to help you add social experiences and build engagement with your apps.

Platform features enable you to engage and connect users.

- **Meta Quest destinations** can be a party, a newly created level for your game, a lobby or user-created world, but by adding destinations to your app, you help users get to where they want to be; either by themselves or with friends.

- **Group presence** is the ability for users to share where they are at in their Meta Quest experience, and once your app has some destinations, you can enable users to share their group presence.

- **Deep links** enable users to get to a destination or where their friends are at.

To learn more about these features, see [Destinations and Group Presence](/documentation/unity/ps-destinations-overview/) and [Deep Linking](/documentation/unity/ps-deep-linking/).

- **Leaderboards** give users something to strive for and see who else is in their community. The platform provides notifications to tell people when their friend has passed their score on a particular leaderboard, this will show up both in VR, in-app, and on the Meta Quest mobile app. By choosing public leaderboards you can help leverage your user's competitive spirit. The following image shows how a public leaderboard might look in the Meta Quest App.

For more about adding leaderboards using the Meta Horizon platform, see [Leaderboards](/documentation/unity/ps-leaderboards/). For leaderboard best practices, see [Best Practices](/documentation/unity/ps-leaderboards/#best-practices/).

## Personalized Social Presence {#personal}

 <img width="200" src="/images/expressive-avatars.jpg" />
Realistic avatars and movements help users feel immersed in your experience.

- **Avatars** make for a believable representation of a person; you don't need to match someone's appearance, but you can match movements and behavior to create a compelling experience. Meta Quest offers expressive Avatars that can match your user's movements and micro expressions such as blinking.

- Use **Lipsync** to help make mouth movements more realistic and match speech. For more information, see [Lipsync](/documentation/unity/audio-ovrlipsync-unity/).

- **Spatialized Audio** helps make conversations and experiences immersive and believable. For more information, see [Audio overview](/documentation/unity/audio-intro/).

## Social Marketing Features {#marketing}

<img width="200" src="/images/dev-posts.png" />

You can create the following opportunities for users to engage in your app.
- Use **Developer Posts** to announce new content in your app. Combine this feature with destinations and deep links to enable users to launch there together, and users now have the ability to create their own events so their friends can join them. For more information, see [Developer Posts](/documentation/unity/ps-developer-posts/).
- Create **Developer Events** that enable users to congregate in your app. Think game nights or leagues. For more information, see [Developer Events](/documentation/unity/ps-events/).

In addition, you can empower users to bring other users to your app experiences using Meta Quest features.
- Enable users to **make friends** in your app by sending friend requests from your apps for Meta Quest. For more information, see [Enable Friend Requests](/documentation/unity/ps-friend-requests-from-apps/).
- Users can create **User events** and invite their friends.
- Make sure your app is opted in to **content sharing** for users to share content. For more information, see [Sharing API](/documentation/unity/ps-sharing/).
