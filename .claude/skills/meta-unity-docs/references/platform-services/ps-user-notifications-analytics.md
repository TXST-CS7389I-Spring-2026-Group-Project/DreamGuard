## Analytics

The user notifications dashboard shows how many people you've reached and their engagement with your notifications. To access analytics, sign in to the [Meta Horizon Developer Dashboard](/manage) and navigate to **Engagement** > **User Notifications**. Select your app in the dropdown menu, then click **View Analytics**.

The following metrics are available:

* **Name**: The name of the notification.
* **Date Sent**: The first date your notification was sent to users.
* **Type**: The type of notification, such as single send or event-based.
* **Number Sent**: The number of notifications sent to unique users.
* **Reach**: An [estimate](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/181058782494426#estimated) of the number of unique users reached by a notification at least once during a given period. Excludes push notifications. Reach can be influenced by users' notification preferences, as well as delivery models that consider which notifications are most relevant to a user at a given time.
* **Clicks**: The number of unique users who clicked on the notification.
* **Clickthrough rate**: The percentage of reached users who clicked the notification (unique clicks divided by reach).
* **Unsubscribes**: The number of users who unsubscribed from the notification.
