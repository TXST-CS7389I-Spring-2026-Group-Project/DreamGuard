## What is Meta Quest Developer Hub?

Meta Quest Developer Hub (MQDH) is an application for Windows and MacOS that allows developers to manage their devices, debug their apps, and submit their apps to the Store.

Features of Meta Quest Developer Hub:

- Viewing the device logs to help with debugging.
- Capturing screenshots and recording videos of what you see in the headset.
- Deploying apps directly to your headset from your computer.
- Sharing your VR experience by casting the headset display to the computer.
- Downloading the latest Meta Quest tools and SDKs.
- Uploading apps to the [Meta Horizon Developer Dashboard](/manage/) for store distribution.
- Disabling the proximity sensor and the boundary system for an uninterrupted testing workflow.
