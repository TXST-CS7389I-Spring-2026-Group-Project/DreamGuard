title: "Add, Remove, and Switch Between Multi-User Accounts"
description: "Manage multiple user accounts on Meta Quest headsets for testing with secondary and test users."
---

You can add, remove, log in to, and switch between multiple secondary accounts on your device, which is especially useful for using [test user accounts](/resources/test-users/). This feature currently has limitations and certain MQDH features may not be supported for secondary users.

This feature does not currently support:

* Signing in with an account not linked to Facebook
* Signing into accounts with 2-factor authentication

## Add New User

**Note**: Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on both the headset and the device running MQDH before adding a secondary account. The account setup process requires a Bluetooth connection to complete.

To add a new user, do the following:

1. From the **Device Manager** tab, click on the drop-down menu to the right of the image of the Quest. It should be labeled with the name of the currently signed-in account.

	

2. Select **Add Account** from the drop-down menu.

3. Once MQDH connects to the Quest, log in with a test user email and password.

New users can also be added from the **Device Settings** modal:

1. Click the gear icon to open the **Device Settings** modal.

	

2. Click the **Accounts** tab.

    

3. Click **Add Account**.

4. Once MQDH connects to the Quest, log in with a test user email and password.

## Delete a User

To delete a user, do the following:

1. Click the gear icon to open the **Device Settings** modal.

2. Click the **Accounts** tab.

3. Click the garbage can icon next to the user's name.

	

4. Click the **Remove** button to remove the account.

	

## Switch Accounts

To switch accounts, click on the drop-down menu to the right of the image of the Quest and click the account you want to switch to.

You can also switch accounts from the **Device Settings** modal:

1. Click the gear icon to open the **Device Settings** modal.
2. Click the **Accounts** tab.
3. Click **Sign in** next to the user you'd like to sign in as.
