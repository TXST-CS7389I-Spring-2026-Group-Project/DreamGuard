## Overview

The **LocomotionExamples** scene demonstrates how to move around the space by teleporting, turning, sliding and stepping.

## How to get the sample

The LocomotionExamples scene can be found in the Unity **Project** window under **Assets** > **Samples** > **Meta XR Interaction SDK** > **VERSION** > **Example Scenes**.

## How to use the sample

* When using hands, enter teleport mode by tapping your thumb. Then aim and tap again to teleport or swipe to turn and step.
* When using controllers, use the left controller to slide around the space, and the right controller to turn, jump and crouch.
* Open the Locomotion setting using the menu button on your non-dominant hand. Use these settings to explore other locomotion options.
