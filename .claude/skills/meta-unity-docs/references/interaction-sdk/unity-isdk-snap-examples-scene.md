## Overview

The **SnapExamples** scene demonstrates how objects can snap to predefined locations, such as your hands or slots on a board.

## How to get the sample

The SnapExamples scene can be found in the Unity **Project** window under **Assets** > **Samples** > **Meta XR Interaction SDK** > **VERSION** > **Example Scenes**.

**Note**: You might need to import the sample from Interaction SDK's Sample section in Unity Package Manager.

## How to use the sample

* Pick up a planet and hover it over one of the empty slots on the rotating board. When a slot is selected, it will light up.
* Release the planet and watch it snap into the slot you selected.
